COMPANY HISTORY

Great things are done by a series of small things brought together. ~ Vincent Van Gogh

2023
We are one of the branch from Tehnik Abadi Group based in Kenari and started our Glodok branch selling Cable Tray and Master Agent for Belden products and Supreme Cables

As a proud member of the Tehnik Abadi Group, we are honored to be a sister company of Trayindo and Traytek. Our journey is intertwined with the rich legacy of Tehnik Abadi, a name synonymous with excellence in the industry.

Founded on the principles of innovation, quality, and unwavering commitment, our company has become a key player in the cable management sector. We specialize in the production of Cable Trays, distributing them across Indonesia with precision and expertise.

In addition to our expertise in cable tray manufacturing, we've diversified our portfolio through our technology company, focusing on Coaxial and LAN cables. As a trusted distributor of Bluetti Portable Power Stations, we bring cutting-edge technology to our customers, ensuring they have reliable power solutions.

Being a part of Tehnik Abadi Group, we carry forward a legacy of excellence, constantly adapting to new technologies and innovations.
